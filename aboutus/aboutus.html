Our Journey

Top Spin was founded in 2010 by visionary coach and tennis enthusiast Herbert Herbertson. With a lifelong love for the sport and an unwavering belief that every player, regardless of skill level, deserves high-quality training, Herbertson laid the foundation for what is today one of the region’s premier tennis academies.

began his journey as a promising junior player, but it was his passion for developing others that came to define his life’s work. With just a single court and a group of dedicated students, he started Top Spin with a simple philosophy: "Every player has potential – it is our job to help them find it."

Over the years, Herbertson ran the academy with unwavering dedication, and his innovative training methods attracted players from all over the country. Through his combination of technical expertise, personal commitment, and a deep understanding of the game’s psychology, he created a culture of continuous improvement and true love for the sport.

His legacy lives on in every serve, every shot, and every player who continues to grow on our courts. Today, we honor Herbertson by carrying his vision forward – offering world-class training, cutting-edge analysis tools, and an environment where every player can reach their full potential. Herbert Herbertson may be gone, but his passion lives on in every ball hit at Top Spin..
